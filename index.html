Thrive - Side by Side

Millions of students leave school each year searching for a purpose. Thrive helps young people connect with elite professionals and ignite their passions.

Help Students Thrive

Our mission is to connect students with the experts they admire most. Every child has a dream and a goal, but the path to success often remains hidden. When students study the values and mindsets of the greatest professionals in their fields, those avenues become more clear. Here at Thrive, we work to help students find their purpose, and get them started on achieving their dreams.

Power and Passion at the Fingertips of Students

Light the Way

Through storytelling, film, and technology, students are exposed to the most illustrious game changers and develop their own story, dreams, and passions.

Find the Jewels

By working with students to identify the key elements that brought these professionals success, Thrive shows the next generation of leaders what it takes to be successful.

Dream Big

When students discover their passion, the world is theirs. Thrive coaches students on best practices in communication and outreach skills that they need to succeed.

Made With Love

We're here to nurture young people, foster their natural abilities, and promote a lifestyle rich with empathy, gratitude, love, and many other important qualities.
